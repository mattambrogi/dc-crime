About

Annual homicides have almost doubled in DC in the past 5 years. This simple web page is intended to bring greater transparency to this issue.

The numbers displayed on this page are derived by collecting alerts sent out by the DC Police Department on Twitter. They are aggregated daily and shown here only as a means of greater transparency. For official stats please visit the Metropolitan Police Deparment's offical website.
